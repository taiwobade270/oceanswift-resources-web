About Us

Oceanswift Resources Nig. Ltd is a Nigerian logistics and supply-chain company founded in 2023. We specialize in international shipping, freight forwarding, and cargo management. Headquartered in Lagos (Ikeja), our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and swift delivery across borders. We leverage global networks and decades of industry expertise to ensure your goods reach their destination on time and in perfect condition.

Our Services

Sea & Air Freight Forwarding

Comprehensive logistics for international shipments by sea and air. We handle all customs and handling to ensure your cargo moves smoothly.

Customs Clearance & Brokerage

Expert customs clearance services to minimize delays and compliance issues. We are licensed customs brokers with deep knowledge of Nigerian and international regulations.

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure warehousing facilities and efficient distribution solutions. Your inventory is stored safely and dispatched promptly as needed.

Consolidation & LCL

Cost-effective consolidation of cargo (LCL) for smaller shipments. We combine your goods with others to save on shipping costs.

Trucking & Haulage

Reliable inland transport for any cargo type. Our insured trucking services cover container haulage and special cargo delivery across Nigeria.

Certifications & Licenses

  • Nigerian Customs Service Licensed Freight Forwarder
  • Member of NAGAFF (Nigerian Association of Government Approved Freight Forwarders)
  • ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Certified
  • Licensed by the Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) for port operations
  • Licensed by the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority (NCAA) for air cargo
