About Us

Oceanswift Resources Nig. Ltd is a licensed clearing and forwarding company with operations at Apapa Port, Tin Can Island Port, Onne Port, and Murtala Muhammed International Airport. Led by CEO Oyebade Taiwo John, with nearly two decades of experience, we specialize in import/export, logistics, and general merchandise. We began with Iconbond Resources Ltd and expanded into Oceanswift to meet modern trade demands. We offer end-to-end solutions that ensure smooth and efficient cargo movement across borders.
